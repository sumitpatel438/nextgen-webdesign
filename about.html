ABOUT US

Get to know Sumit's Store - your trusted source for premium gaming controllers and accessories.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by gaming enthusiast Sumit Patel, Sumit's Store began with a simple mission: to provide gamers with the highest quality controllers and accessories to enhance their gaming experience.

What started as a small online shop has grown into a trusted destination for gamers looking for premium products, exceptional customer service, and competitive prices. Our passion for gaming drives us to continuously seek out the best products and stay up-to-date with the latest gaming technology.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers across the country, helping them elevate their gaming experience with our carefully curated selection of controllers, headsets, and other gaming accessories.

Our Mission

To provide gamers with premium quality controllers and accessories that enhance their gaming experience, backed by exceptional customer service and competitive prices.

Our Vision

To become the leading destination for gaming controllers and accessories, recognized for our quality products, innovation, and dedication to customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Quality, Integrity, Customer Service, Innovation, and Passion for Gaming. These core values guide everything we do at Sumit's Store.
